How to assemble a gaming chair

Parts of a gaming chair

  • Head rest
  • Caster wheels
  • RGB LED lights
  • Armrest mechanism
  • Lumbar Support
  • Tilt mechanism
  • Gas lift spring

Steps-by-Step Assembly

  1. Unpack everything in the box
    2. =You ordered your gaming chair from amazon or lazada its time to unpack. Be careful when opening the box with a knife.
  2. Lay out the materials in orderly fasion.
    3. =When you counted all the parts needed remove the plastic covers and lay them in a orderly fashion so that you won't lose any parts and easy to help assemble the gaming chair.
  3. Connect the main chair mechanism with the underside of the chair.
    4. =When connecting the mechanism to the underside use 4 screw head bolts and tightly both the chair. This step will help the chair carry your weight.
  4. Attach the lumbar support and armrest mechanism
    5. =The lumbar support is a strap-on so its easy to place and as for the arm rest you need to bolt them with screws.
  5. Put the caster wheels under the gas lift mechanism
    6. =Use bolts to tighten the caster wheels to the gas lift mechanism
  6. Attach the head rest and RGB lights
    7. =The headrest is also a strap-on where you can strap it into your chair. As for the lights there are holes under the headrest place the 2 lights on the holes and your all set. It turns on automatically when placed
  7. Finished
    8. =You finally assembled your chair its time to kick back and relax.

After the assembly

  1. Do not jump on the chair because too much preasure might break it.
  2. Do not spill any food or beverages on the chair
  3. Use rags to wipe of the dust on the chair or compressed air can blow out the dirt.
  4. Replace any parts of the chair if any are broken.
