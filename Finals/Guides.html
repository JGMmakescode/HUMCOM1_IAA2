WHAT IS VR (VIRTUAL REALITY)

It is a term used to describe a 3-dimensional, computer generated environment which can be explored and interacted with by a person. The user becomes part of the generated virtual world where he/she is immersed with the environment while interacting with the objects. It makes the user believe they're world where they can use their imagination.





THE BASICS

The most important equipment is the headset. It is a device like a thick pair of goggles that you wear it for head and eyes. It also comes with hand controllers to feel and touch the virtual world. The hand controllers come with straps so that it would not fall on the floor. When you buy our VR devices from our store it is affordable and has higher quality.
